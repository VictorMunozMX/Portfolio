Boomer is the first pet we have as a family and now we consider him one more member. He was born on December 29, 2020, he was the youngest of his litter and the only one gray, his brothers are brown, his mother is light brown and his father is black.

Everyone in the family loves him very much. We saw him grow up from a young age; we heard his first bark, and his peculiar way of walking makes him unique.

now, we have learned a pet is not only an animal, it is like a son or brother instead is a member of the family, it has its needs, you have to give it a walk, you have to feed it and you have to give it love. Remember with great joy when it was his first shot and he kept his leg straight like a human, which was fun. Soon I will tell more stories now in Canada, however, he enjoys his walks a lot.
