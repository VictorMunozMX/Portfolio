Mazatlan

Jun 15, 2021

Mazatlán is a Mexican resort town along the Pacific shoreline in the state of Sinaloa. Sandy beaches line its 21km-long malecón (boardwalk), and it's renowned for big-game fishing. In its Centro Histórico, or Old Mazatlán, 19th-century landmarks include the performance hall Teatro Ángela Peralta and the towering Immaculate Conception basilica. The modern district of Zona Dorada is known for nightlife and hotels.

An experience to enjoy an excellent summer in the company of your family or friends is Mazatlan. Now, it is one of my favourite places, it has beaches for activities such as surfing, fishing, or simply swimming with moderate and safe waves.<\p>

Furthermore, there are several places to visit such as the golden zone, the lighthouse, a trip on the pulmonia, to know the island of the stone, to know the amazing beaches such as deer beach, witches beach, north beach, high waves and why not, to know the magical towns around it.
