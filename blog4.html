Victor Munoz

Canada

Nov 10, 2022

As background, my origin country is very hot and the temperatures are higher than 5 degrees, therefore, I would not have had this experience in Mexico. Now in Canada, it gives the opportunity to enjoy this experience and it was exciting, pleasant, in other words, amazing.

My wife and daughters and even our mascot Boomer enjoyed it a lot, that day was incredible. I consider like this there are other experiences that would be pleasant to live. The only drawback was the time it lasted, 1 day, now we are waiting for the next one to be able to create our family snowman because since we had activities like school and work it was impossible..

This experience brought back my childhood. In the Nevado de Toluca in Mexico we went with my parents, it is a mountain and there is snow on the tip. Unfortunately, the heights made my pressure drop and from being pleasant to climb to the top it became martyrdom, my parents had to carry me down because I lost consciousness, hahaha now it sounds fun but I imagine it was not pleasant for my parents.
